Help & Support

Find answers to common questions or contact our support team

How do I reset my password?

Go to your Account settings and click "Reset Password". You will receive an email with further instructions.

How can I contact support?

You can contact our support team via email at support@example.com or use the live chat in the app.

How do I delete my account?

Please contact support to request account deletion. Your data will be permanently removed after confirmation.
